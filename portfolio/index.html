Scooby Doo Map

Ellie Towns

This explainer describes a web map based on the groovy, cartoonish style of the Scooby Doo cartoons.
(With a spooky twist!)

This map draws on the retro style of the old Scooby Doo cartoons while utilizing its famous color scheme of bright greens, oranges, blues and purples. It also takes inspiration from scenes at night, extracted from the wispy trees and shadows.
