Scooby Doo Themed Tileset

Artist Statement




Growing up, I loved the old Scooby Doo cartoons as well as the more recent movies. When given the opportunity to create a themed tileset on Mapbox, my mind immediately went to Scooby Doo. The vibrant colors and retro style seemed like it would create such a good visual style. I loved incorporating some spooky elements as well to symbolize the premise of the show, which are the villains.
